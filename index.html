What is STEM Racing?

STEM Racing is a global educational challenge where students design, build, and race miniature F1-style cars. It combines science, technology, engineering and maths in a hands-on, high-performance format that prepares the next generation of innovators.

Our Car

Our car is built for speed and precision on the track. It features lift-generating design elements that reduce friction between the wheels and the surface, allowing for faster, smoother movement. Lightweight and aerodynamic, it's engineered to meet regulations while maximizing efficiency. Every detail balances performance with style, making it a standout at competitions.

Explore the Car

Manufacturing:

Our car is designed in Fusion 360 where the design is exported for manufacturing. The STEM Racing model block is cut using a CNC machine, while lightweight parts are 3D printed in PLA. We use bearings to ensure low friction and high RPM, and carbon fiber axles for strength and lightness. Tiny neodymium magnets in the front wing make it easily replaceable. All parts are carefully painted and stickers applied before precise assembly, ensuring both durability and a polished finish.
